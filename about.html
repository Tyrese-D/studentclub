About Our Club

A place for Rutgers students to connect and grow. Helping students gain build websites, strengthening development skills.

Who We Are

We are a student-run organization dedicated to helping students build community, improve professional skills and website development, and get involved on campus. Giving student opportunities to strengthen skills and gain experience.

Learn More

The club was created to give Rutgers students a stronger campus community and more opportunities to learn outside of class. Helping students get more experience and building websites.

Members can attend events, hear announcements, meet other students, and get access to club resources.

You can attend an event, use the contact page, and ask to be added as a member.
