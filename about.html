About Our Club

A place for Rutgers students to connect and grow.

Who We Are

We are a student-run organization dedicated to helping students build community, improve professional skills, and get involved on campus.

Learn More

The club was created to give Rutgers students a stronger campus community and more opportunities to learn outside of class.

Members can attend events, hear announcements, meet other students, and get access to club resources.

You can attend an event, use the contact page, and ask to be added as a member.
